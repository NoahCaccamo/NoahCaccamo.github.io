NOAH CACCAMO

gigaWorld();

Showcased at the Ontario Science Centre.

Created by: Noah Caccamo, Mariana De Queiroz, Sabrina Leung, Yasmin Camas, Shay Pinkse

gigaWorld(); is an interactive environmental simulation. Within this transparent display, you can watch and participate in the emergence of new life.

You can control the temperature and humidity with knobs and buttons to create new environments. In these environments, you can incubate eggs and plant seeds to bring new creatures and plants to life. They thrive in their current environment, but changes in climate can affect them greatly.

gigaWorld(); aims to spark conversation about how climate change affects life on this planet.
