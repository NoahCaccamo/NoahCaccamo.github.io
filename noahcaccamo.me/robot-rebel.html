Robot Rebel

Robot Rebel is a rogue-like prototype made in a game design class. Slash and shoot through a randomly generated dungeon finding upgrades along the way. Careful placement of time-slowing fields is crucial for both defense and offense.
Created by: Noah Caccamo, Sabrina Leung and Tyler Tran
